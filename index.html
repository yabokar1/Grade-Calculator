Decimal GPA Calculator

This is a GPA calculator that conforms to the decimal grading system in use by certain colleges. The class/course name field is just for reference and is not required. Rows with the grade and/or credits fields that are blank are ignored. Grades entered are not saved.

Omit grades that are not a decimal, such as but are not limited to: I, W, *, Y, NC, N, WI, R, CR
